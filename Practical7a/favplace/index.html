Mudal - My village aka The village of Trucks

Village of trucks? It sounds awkward right. But its true for my village. Because there is atleast one truck in each home. Due to this people got some sources to earn. Also because of these many people got employeement in occupations like drivers, auto garages and other stuffs. People in village also have concentrated on agriculture . Due to canal facility and fertile soil most of the people producing sugar cane. Due to icreasing demad of sugar , this village is surrounded by many sugar factories. One of the nearest sugar factory is Bidri sugar factory.
